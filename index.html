Our mission is to bring back the "Search Directory". Back in the early days of the internet before fancier search engines took over, there were curated link directories broken out by subject. What we are hoping to build is the next generation of this type of search directory. By backing LinkCred you can help bring back the quality and human intelligence to finding new and interesting information on the internet.

Even though we harken back to some of the earliest forms of search on the internet, this isn't a replay of 1994. We are creating a system to allow you, our users, to build and curate the directory as a community. There are many more suprises on how this will all work coming in the near future. We have several exciting ideas that can be used to make LinkCred fun and useful and look forward to showing some sneak peeks soon.

FAQ

How is LinkCred diffent than the old Yahoo Directory?
Great question. The primary problem with the old style of internet directory was scalability. Since it was internally curated it required a large staff of people to make sure it was up to date and included relevent links. By allowing you to edit or recommend edits to the directory, this will be much easier. This might sound very simple and it is, but we are leaving a few details out.
I'm pretty sure I've seen this before. Are you sure this is new?
The basic concept has been tried many times with different degree's of success. However, there are some very significant differences around how the community works and how the directory will operate that we believe creates a unique, fun, and useful experience.
